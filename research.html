Research Output

My research investigates urban–environment systems using spatial modeling, remote sensing, and geospatial analytics. The roadmap below illustrates the evolution of my research trajectory, from the development of spatial indicators for urban morphology and land-use dynamics toward integrated modeling of urban climate systems, environmental processes, and spatial data science. Early work (2021–2024) focused on spatial metrics for analyzing urban structure, dispersion, and land-use transformation. Recent studies (2025–2026) extend these approaches toward modeling urban environmental systems, including climate mitigation, ecological corridor analysis, and geospatial big-data analytics. This trajectory ultimately supports my long-term research vision toward **urban systems modeling for climate-resilient and low-carbon development**.
