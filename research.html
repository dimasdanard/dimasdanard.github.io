Research Output

Welcome to my research trajectory overview. My research focuses on urban–environment systems, integrating spatial modeling, geospatial analytics, and remote sensing to understand land-use transformation, environmental dynamics, and climate risks in rapidly urbanizing regions. The roadmap below illustrates how my research evolved from early work on urban spatial structure and land-use dynamics toward broader themes of environmental risk assessment, climate resilience, and sustainable urban systems.
