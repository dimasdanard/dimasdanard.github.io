Research Output

My research explores the dynamics of urban–environment systems using spatial modeling, geospatial analytics, and remote sensing. I investigate how land-use transformation, urban spatial structure, and environmental processes interact within rapidly growing metropolitan regions. This work integrates spatial data science, landscape analysis, and environmental risk assessment to support climate-resilient and low-carbon urban development.
