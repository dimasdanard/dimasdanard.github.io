Research Output

My research investigates urban–environment systems using spatial modeling, remote sensing, and geospatial analytics. The roadmap below illustrates the evolution of my research themes and scholarly outputs from early work on urban spatial structure and land-use dynamics toward broader topics in environmental risk assessment, climate resilience, and spatial data science.

2030
Future Vision — Climate-Resilient Urban Systems
Urban systems modeling for climate-resilient and low-carbon development, integrating ecological corridor networks, spatial big data, and urban climate risk analytics.
2026
Geospatial Data Science & Spatial Analytics
(J1) Exploratory spatial co-patterns of VIIRS nighttime radiance and land cover compositional evenness at 10-km resolution in Java, Indonesia.
2025
Urban Environmental Systems & Climate Mitigation
(J2) A Framework for Optimizing Open Spatial Data in Urban Planning and Policy Applications.
(J3) Remote sensing-based investigation of outlying urban expansion and its impact on landscape diversity in the peri-urban area of Semarang City.
(J4) Impact of urbanization on carbon emissions and ecological quality in the Semarang Metropolitan Region.
(J5) Integrating Google Earth Engine and ecological corridor modeling for urban heat island mitigation.
(J6) Spatiotemporal variations of urban expansion and its relation with geomorphological complexity.
2024
Urban Ecological Dynamics
(J7) Spatiotemporal variations of urban spatial dispersion and its vegetation degradation in Joglosemar’s urban growth corridors.
2023
Urban Environmental Impacts
(J8) Modifying the contact perimeter approach for measuring urban compactness gradients.
(J9) Impacts of rapid urbanization on spatial dynamics of land-use-based carbon emission and surface temperature changes.
2022
Urban Spatial Structure & Land-Use Dynamics
(J10) Assessing land use/land cover change diversity and its relation with urban dispersion using Shannon Entropy.
(J11) Shannon Entropy-based urban spatial fragmentation to ensure sustainable development of coastal cities.
2021
Urban Spatial Analysis Foundations
(C1) Assessment of rapid urban development impact for a small-sized transit city using remote sensing: A case study of Salatiga.
