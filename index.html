Dr. Dimas Danar Dewa
Urban Systems & Spatial Modeling
Modeling Urban–Environment Dynamics for Climate-Resilient and Sustainable Development

Welcome to my homepage.

I am a Research Fellow at the National Research and Innovation Agency (BRIN), Indonesia. My research integrates spatial modeling, GIScience, and remote sensing to examine urban–environment systems, ecological dynamics, and environmental change in rapidly transforming metropolitan regions.

I focus on developing data-driven approaches for disaster risk reduction, environmental risk assessment, and adaptive planning, contributing to sustainable and low-carbon urban transitions in emerging economies.
