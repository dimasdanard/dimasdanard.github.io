Dr. Dimas Danar Dewa
Urban Systems & Spatial Modeling
Modeling Urban–Environment Dynamics for Climate-Resilient and Sustainable Development

Welcome to my homepage.

I am a Research Fellow at the National Research and Innovation Agency (BRIN), Indonesia, specializing in advanced spatial modeling, GIScience, and remote sensing.

My work integrates geospatial analytics and urban-environment modeling to understand how land-use dynamics, urban form, and socio-environmental processes shape rapidly transforming metropolitan regions.

I focus on strengthening urban resilience through data-driven approaches to disaster risk reduction, environmental risk assessment, and adaptive planning — contributing to sustainable and low-carbon urban transitions, particularly in emerging economies.
