Academic Profile

Dr. Dimas Danar Dewa is a researcher specializing in urban spatial modeling, GIScience, and remote sensing for understanding urban–environment dynamics and climate-resilient urban development. His research integrates spatial analytics, geospatial big data, and environmental modeling to investigate how land-use dynamics, urban form, and socio-environmental processes shape rapidly transforming metropolitan regions.
